### 3.2 Hook portability (HIGH — issue #68)

`hooks/hooks.json`:

```json
"command": "python3 \"${CLAUDE_PLUGIN_ROOT}/hooks/validate-schema.py\" \"$FILE_PATH\""
```

Windows ships with `python.exe`, not `python3.exe`. Result: hook fails silently on
every Edit/Write for 100% of Windows users. Two viable fixes:

1. Change to `python` and rely on PATH ordering.
2. Use a shell wrapper that resolves `python3 || python` (more brittle on Windows; not recommended).

Suggest option 1.

Also: `hooks/validate-schema.py` lacks a docstring and there's no `hooks/README.md`
explaining what it validates. Users can't tell what quality gate is being enforced.
